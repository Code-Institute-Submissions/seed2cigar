Welcome

Welcome to 'Seed 2 Cigars' where you can come and learn all about the planting, harvesting and manufacturing of these amazing creations we call cigars. There are so many cigar review sites and cigar retailers online. However, we know that there are cigar people out there that want to dive deeper into the life of a cigar. What seeds are chosen to be planted, to how the plants are grown and transferred to the fields. When is the best time for harvesting the cigar leaves. Even down to the production can be quite a journey. We have compiled a lot of our information into this website for users like you to learn this amazing journey to help better improve your cigar knowledge and enjoyment. As you go through this cigar journey we hope the interactivity of the site will keep you on your toes and help you learn. Before we set you free, don't forget that if you are ever stuck with anything cigar related please feel free to get in touch with us and we always aim to reply to your questions withing 24 hours. Thank you and enjoy your journey.
